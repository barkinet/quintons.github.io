My name is Quinton, a Contractor interested in all things web related from design to development.

 

The Service

As a contractor, I provide my expertise as an experienced front end developer to companies across a diverse range of sectors from the financial to retail, integrating within small to large teams of developers. I pride myself in being able to collaborate with various types of teams from designers to developers to testers and business stakeholders, understanding their objectives and pushing to deliver on their expectations.

During any project development cycle I focus my attention on code maintainability, reusability and scalability as well as performance and the workflow processes within the team(s). Initially any architectural challenges that a front-end project faces are primarily, in essence a problem with a solution, finding that solution to suit the project is a first step towards starting on the correct footing.
